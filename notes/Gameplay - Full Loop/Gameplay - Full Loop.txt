[ROOM] Menu
	Game Start
	Display Introduction Text
[ROOM] Tower Defense
	Place towers
	Signal Enemy wave
	Wave completes
	Option to "scavenge for resources" between waves (maybe with a cooldown of every 3 waves?)
Survival Minigame
	Kill as many wizards as you can in the allotted time
	Gain resources depending on which wizards you target
	When the timer runs out you return to [ROOM] Tower Defense

Win condition:
	You survive until the last wave, which happens to have the second-last wizard included in the wave
	Killing the mini-boss results in a winning game
	
Lose condition:
	Mercenaries reach your tower and take you out -> DEFEAT.
	Wizards somehow kill you during survival minigame -> DEFEAT.
